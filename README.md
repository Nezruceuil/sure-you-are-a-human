# sure-you-are-a-human

Is a fast-clicking game built in Godot,to prove you're human as fast as possible !

There are achivements that you can get by having low scores or doing secret stuff!

## About

sure-you-are-a-human is a simple but addictive speed-click game. A button labeled **"I'm a human"** appears on screen — click it,
then hit **"Submit"** as quickly as you can. Your time is recorded each level, and the goal is to keep it as low as possible.

## How to Play

1. Go to https://nezruceuil.itch.io/sureyourahuman
2. Do the intro like how to do the level (below)
3. Go to level select a level
4. Click on the **"I'm a human"** button or don't click on the **"I'm a Robot"** button
5. Hit **Submit** to confirm
6. and redo it 14 times
7. Your time is recorded — lowest time is better!

> **Tip:** Need help figuring something out ? **Bobling** go to Bobling in the advancements tab
